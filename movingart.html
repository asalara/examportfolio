Moving art is about transforming a still image into an animated image. This requires skills in photoshop, animateCC and a vivid imagination. The task in this workshop was to create a story that revolves around a piece of art of our own choice and visualising it through an animation.

I decided to go with Picasso and his blue period. The paintings are not the typical Picasso style most people know. They are from 1901-1904 when he painted essentially monochromatic paintings in shades of blue and blue-green. These paintings have always effected my, the colour, melancolic feeling and painting style is what I am mostly drawn to.

The story behind Picassos "Blue periode" moved me and I thought I could create a visual story with animating a selection of the paintings. The story is about the death of Picassos friend, Casagemas. He was in love with a women who did not love him back so he shoot himself after trying to shoot her first. This woman later became PicassoÂ´s lover.
